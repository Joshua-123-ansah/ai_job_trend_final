AI and the Future of Work

Visualizing Job Creation and Job Loss

Abraham Troop, Josh Ebe, Shawn Neill, Eeman Huda, Gabriel Ilano, Joshua Owusu Ansah

abrahamm@asu.edu, jebe1@asu.edu, sneill1@asu.edu, ehuda@asu.edu, gilano@asu.edu, jowusuan@asu.edu

Artificial Intelligence has been a popular topic recently due to its rapid increases in advancements and popularity. Particularly, AI has been a topic of interest in the workplace because of its efficiency and ability to automate jobs. AI impact in the workplace depends heavily on industry, skill level, and business decision making. When evaluating AI's impact on jobs, it is important to consider if AI is causing growth, decline, or no impact on jobs. To determine how jobs are being impacted by AI, it is important to evaluate which job domains are impacted, how much they are impacted, and what education is required for that job.
